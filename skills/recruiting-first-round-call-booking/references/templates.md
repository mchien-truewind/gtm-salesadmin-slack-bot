# Recruiting Round-1 Templates

## Draft Email Template (Singular + Timezone)

```text
Hi {{first_name}},

I scheduled a time on {{weekday}} at {{time_with_tz}}. Let me know if it doesn't work for you and we can adjust.

Best,
Mercedes
```

## Subject-to-Event Title Sanitization

1. Remove `[hiring@]` exactly.
2. Remove leading `Re:` and `Fwd:` (repeat until none remain).
3. Collapse extra spaces.
4. Trim leading/trailing punctuation and spaces.
